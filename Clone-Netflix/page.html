SCHINDLER´S LIST

German Oskar Schindler saw in Jewish labor a cheap and viable solution to profit from business during the war. With his strong influence within the Nazi party, it was easy to get permits and open a factory. What might seem like an attitude of a not very kind man, turned into one of the greatest love affairs in history, as this German abdicated all his fortune to save the lives of more than a thousand Jews amid struggle against the Nazi extermination.
